# Product Positioning Strategy — Campus to Corporate (c2c)

This document establishes the positioning architecture for **c2c** as a standalone, productized SaaS platform designed for high-scale placement operations.

---

## 1. The Core Wedge: High-Volume, Low-Cost Placement Analytics
Traditional recruitment solutions rely heavily on high-touch consulting services or manual screening. **c2c** differentiates by being a focused, automation-first software product:

- **Purpose-Built for Volume:** Designed to run assessments and matchings for batches of 1,500+ students simultaneously.
- **Serverless Cost Efficiency:** The platform is engineered on a serverless backend. The total operational cost per student profile is **under $0.15** (comprising database queries, static rendering, and deterministic scoring).
- **Automation over Services:** We do not offer bespoke, custom integration consulting. The product delivers standard, high-performance dashboards for Training and Placement Officers (TPOs) and Employers out-of-the-box.

---

## 2. Product Focus Decisions

### Out-of-Scope: Coding Sandboxes
- **Strategic Focus:** c2c focuses on mapping **behavioral and cognitive alignment** (using IQ, EQ, SQ, AQ, and SpQ dimensions) to **founder archetypes** (Builder, Leader, Rainmaker, Anchor).
- **Market Differentiation:** High-fidelity in-browser code execution environments (like LeetCode or HackerRank clones) are commoditized. Instead of duplicating a developer environment, c2c maps how candidates communicate, handle stress, structure their career stories, and align with company values.

### Deferrals: FutureHub Marketplace
- The marketplace layer (FutureHub) is deferred as a **Phase 4+ roadmapped item**. To maintain a tight GTM wedge, near-term development prioritizes placement metrics, archetype mapping, and deterministic job matching.
