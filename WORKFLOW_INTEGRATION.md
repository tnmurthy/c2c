### 3. Service Integration Matrix

#### **A. Phase 1: Signal & Evidence**
*   **Input:** Raw Candidate CV / LinkedIn / GitHub URL.
*   **Processor:** `job-intel-desk` identifies market demand; `git-optimizer` audits the "Truth of the Code."
*   **Output:** **Audit Report** (Ranking + Remediation List).

#### **B. Phase 2: Behavioral Validation**
*   **Input:** Audit Report + Targeted Role Spec.
*   **Processor:** `agent-recruiters` (The Academic & Engineering Swarm) performs a deep-dive interview simulation.
*   **Output:** **Psychological & Cultural Profile** (Strength/Weakness Map).

#### **C. Phase 3: The Makeover**
*   **Input:** Weakness Map from Stage 2.
*   **Processor:** `campus-to-corporate` provides targeted training modules and career readiness drills.
*   **Output:** **Certified Candidate** (Verified Enterprise-Ready).

#### **D. Phase 4: Market Deployment**
*   **Input:** Full Candidate History + Certification.
*   **Processor:** `brand-optimizer` creates the interactive portfolio and "Professional Legend."
*   **Output:** **Live Portfolio URL** (Optimized for conversion).
